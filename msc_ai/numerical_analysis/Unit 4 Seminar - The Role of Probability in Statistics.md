## What is a probability distribution?

A distribution, or more specifically, a probability distribution, is a mathematical function that links each value of a random variable with its probability of occurrence in the range of possible values (the so‐called sample space).

## What are the different types of distribution?

* Discrete distributions
  - Binomial
  - Poisson
  - Negative binomial
* Continous distributions
  - Normal or Gauss
  - Gamma
  - Beta
