To date, the history of computing has been supported by five important and ever-present trends: ubiquity, interconnection, intelligence, delegation, and human-orientation.

A key feature of AI agent is its ability to udertake autonomous actions. An agent does not just theorise, but *carry out* the task to meet the objectives set by the user.

Autonomy is a sprectrum. In case of AI agents, one needs to build in *adjustable autonomy*, wherein the agent will hand the control back to a human in below scenarios.
- When the agent believes that a human will make a better decision.
- When there is a high degree of uncertainty.
- When the decision might cause harm.
- When the agent lacks the capability to decide alone.

Agent's actions are linked to *preconditions*.
